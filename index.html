Who am I?

After many years in coffee roasting and consulting, I've stepped back to roast at a smaller scale — reconnecting with community and the people I care about. This is about great coffee for even greater people.

I'm sourcing some of the most exceptional coffees I can find — exclusive micro and nano lots I'm passionate about, from amazing people. I want to share that journey with you.

This is my passion project. A transitional brand. It won't last forever, so enjoy it while it's here.

If you're here, you're part of my community. Buckle up, have fun.
